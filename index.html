About Geaux Gaming

Framed

Geaux Gaming is a video game design team formed at Louisiana State University in the Spring of 2019. The team consists of multiple programmers and designers in their third and forth year of Computer Science. This Spring, Geaux Gaming will unviel their newest project, a mystery puzzle game focused on engaging young minds to solve a puzzle mystery case!

Meet the Team

Jun Lin

Jun is a senior Computer Science student at LSU. He studies robotics, artificial intelligence, and mathematics as well. Jun has participated in many Global Game Jams at LSU. He is the lead programmer of Geaux Gaming and team leader in the development of Framed!

Elizabeth Alvarado

Elizabeth is our graphic designer here at Geaux Gaming. She has also participated in many Global Game Jams and currently studies Computer Science and Art Design at LSU. Elizabeth is the lead designer of artwork for Geaux Gaming's newest project, Framed!

Aliza Bista

Aliza Bista is a senior at LSU majoring in Computer Science and minoring in Mathematics. She has participate in many WICS Game Jams over the past four years. This Spring, Aliza will be working as one of our main programmers here at Geaux Gaming working on Framed!

Joseph Nguyen

Joseph is a fourth year Computer Science Student at LSU and our web designer here at Geaux Gaming. He has worked with several programming languages and softwares including: Unity, HTML, C#, Java, and Phython. He will assist the team with programming, UI design, and web design for Geaux Gaming.

Ward Leavines

Ward is the UI designer at Geaux Gaming. He has worked on several projects working on software applications ranging to creating a scheduler, parking application, and an Artificial Intelligence controlling the game Pacman. He currently studies Computer Science at LSU, and is working on a project we can commercialize in the near future.
