Phase 2

Phase one is complete and some last minute changes are being made to phase two. Character designs are in the final stages of development. A working demo will be issued very soon. Phase three will tackle the storyline and include all the clues needed to solve the mystery case. More powerups and enemies are being developed as well.
Jun Lin: Jun is the leader of the group, so his primary focus initially was to take our initial game idea and target a structure and design type that he found best. Once these initial steps were completed he contributed to the implement character control and partial AI system, some of level design, and merge all the work into single working master branch. Joseph Nguyen: Joseph initially worked on the life system. This means that he set up three hearts that form the life bar. The player will lose a heart if they are caught by the enemies and if they find a powerup they can gain a heart. He also set the maximum of four hearts. As our primary web developer, when the life system was complete his next task was to update our website. Ward Leavines: Ward was given the responsibility of creating the main menu as well as the pause menu and game over screen. He not only created the menu options for both screens, but also designed the basic design so that way he can iterate on it before we show the final product. Elizabeth Alvarado: Elizabeth was our head art designer. As such, she was responsible for the design of our protagonist Tora as well as the other NPCs and enemies in our game. She also created tiles for the background and walls of each level. She also helped get the group familiar with tile mapping - which is the core featured used in creating the levels within the game. Tyler McDonald: Tyler was given the responsibility of helping with game design and scripting of the levels. He was one of the main creators of the storyline and situations in our game. He contributed some to the scripting of Level one and was also one of the main contributors to the documentation. Aliza Bista: Aliza was given the responsibility of helping with the scripting of the levels. She was the other contributor to the first level and helped with the scripting of the other levels as well. Aliza helped tile the first level.
